About Me

Hello! I'm Nithin Weerasinghe. I'm a recent graduate from the University of Michigan (Go Blue! 〽️) where I double majored in computer science and cognitive science (with a minor in complex systems). I love learning. In my free time, I enjoy podcasts, graphic design, board games, fantasy football, anime, cooking, and just spending time with friends. I'm also a hardcore Detroit Lions fan. My ultimate dream is to contribute to the scientific understanding of the human mind. In addition, I want to use human-computer interaction to apply these insights to improve one’s education, creativity, and mental health. To better tackle these goals, I'm currently applying to graduate programs in computer science (with a focus on artificial intelligence) and human-computer interaction and hope to pursue interdisciplinary research.
